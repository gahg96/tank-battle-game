# Changelog

## [Unreleased] - 2025-11-27

### Added
- **New Weapons**:
    - **Sniper Railgun**: High velocity, high damage, long cooldown.
    - **Missile Launcher**: Area of effect explosive damage.
    - **Weapon Switching**: Press `SHIFT` to cycle between Cannon, Railgun, and Missile.
- **Tank Roles**:
    - **Assault**: Balanced stats, Standard Cannon.
    - **Sniper**: Fast, Low HP, Railgun.
    - **Demolisher**: Slow, High HP, Missile.
- **Squad Variety**: Player squad now always contains one of each role.
- **Enemy Variety**: Enemies spawn with random roles.
- **In-Game Help**: Press `F1` to view controls and game info in Chinese.
- **Teammate AI**:
    - Teammates now move and shoot simultaneously.
    - Teammates respect their role's optimal engagement range (Snipers stay back).

### Changed
- **Controls**:
    - Respawn key changed from `F1` to `ENTER`.
    - Added `SHIFT` for weapon switching.
- **Balance**:
    - Reduced tank rotation speed for better control.
    - Increased standard cannon bullet speed.
    - Bullets now travel the full map distance instead of just screen distance.
